FIELD NOTE · 04 · DOMAIN SPLIT

Four wings, one pursuit: how one mark carries .org, .studio, .com, and .net.

Field notes on running a four-entity practice from a single mark — we build it, prove it, and publish what we learned.

The dragonfly's four wings map to four entities. The body is the pursuit they share: build it, prove it, publish what we learned. Each wing gets to lead with its own color when the surface is entity-specific — research blue for .org, learning blue for .studio, commercial teal for .com, platform green for .net. But the body color stays the same. The body is what the wings have in common, and what visitors should infer about the practice without ever being told.
